About Me

Hi, I’m an aspiring Technical Artist who loves playing in the weird space between art and tech. I dabble in both 2D and 3D, which basically means I can make stuff look cool and overly complicate it with shaders. I’m constantly learning new tools and techniques—partly because I’m curious, partly because, let’s be real, nothing ever stays the same in this industry.

My goal? To keep pushing boundaries, solve problems creatively, and maybe even impress people along the way. If you’re into that kind of thing, we should talk.
