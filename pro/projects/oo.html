Oopz-Oof

A quirky, playful dog sim with a body-swap twist!

Story

Waking up from a sweet dream, John finds himself in his dog's body! Sniff, bark, beg, and use your paw to interact with the townfolks. Can John find a way to return to his human form, or will this unexpected adventure change him forever?
