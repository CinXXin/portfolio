Love Next Door

A Dating Visual Novel... for Doors?

Created for a 48-hour game jam with the theme "Unhinged," Love Next Door is a quirky and hilarious dating visual novel featuring charming, romantic... doors. Players engage in witty dialogue and unexpected twists in their quest for true door love.
