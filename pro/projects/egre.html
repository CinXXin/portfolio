Egregore

A captivating 3D animation project where surrealism meets storytelling. The project explores abstract forms and the relationship between the ethereal and the physical.

My Contributions

I took charge of the 3D modeling, texturing, and shading workflows, including rigging the main character and creating dynamic environments. I also implemented technical solutions to streamline the animation pipeline.

Lessons Learned

This project taught me the importance of iterative workflows and the balance between technical precision and artistic creativity. Developing modular assets for flexibility across multiple scenes proved invaluable.
